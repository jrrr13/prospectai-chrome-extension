How it works

From profile to message
in under 10 seconds.

01
Open a LinkedIn profile
Navigate to any prospect's profile. The side panel opens and reads the page automatically.
02
AI parses the details
GPT-4o-mini extracts the actual job title from the Experience section ‚Äî not the tagline ‚Äî plus company, seniority, and location.
03
Company intel lookup
Searches for recent funding, launches, or news to give the opener a specific hook where possible.
04
Three messages, instantly
Email, LinkedIn DM, and WhatsApp ‚Äî all adapted to the prospect's country, seniority, and your chosen tone. Copy and send.
